Travel Requirements

What you need to cross

Crossing into Canada is straightforward — here's what to bring.
🛂

Accepted Documents

You need one of these to enter Canada:

  • Valid U.S. Passport
  • U.S. Passport Card
  • Enhanced Driver's License (MI, NY, VT, WA, MN)
  • NEXUS Card
🎂

Age Requirements

Ontario's legal drinking age is 19. You must be at least 19 to access nightlife venues on our tours. All travelers must be 18+ to book.

🚫

Border Tips

Smooth crossings every time:

  • No criminal record issues (DUI, etc.)
  • No cannabis — it's illegal to cross with
  • Declare all purchases on return
  • Be polite and honest with officers
🛡️

Safety & Support

Your safety is our top priority. Every trip includes:

  • Tour leader with every group
  • 24/7 emergency contact line
  • Buddy system & headcounts
  • Pre-trip orientation on the bus
